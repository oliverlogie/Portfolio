Education

I started programming at the age of 15. I studied at the Evangelisches Gymnasium & Werkschulheim , where I completed a special education branch called IT-Technician together with my Matura. During these years I had internships in various companies especially on IT-branches. I finished my degree with a School Diploma, as well as an apprenticeship diploma in the Information Technologies sector.

After that I started studying technical chemistry at TU-Wien and working odd jobs at the same time. Eventually, I realized that this was not what I wanted. Hence, I break off my studies and started to focus on programming again. In 2019, I decided to really focus on my programming and learning more about it. Thus I enrolled for an intensive 4-month Fullstack-Junior Web Developer course at Code Factory which I graduated with summa cum laude.

I know that there is a continuous learning in information technology. I am eager to expand my skills, particularly what is needed to be a good Web Developer, because it is my true passion. Programming is not always easy, to say the least, but I love spending time on the computer and find solutions to problems
